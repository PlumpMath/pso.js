pso.js + p2.js
In this example pso.js will be used to try to get as many balls in the pockets as possible in the first/break shot.
The physics simulations are handled by the physics library p2.js

The only parameters that a simulation takes are the angle of the shot and the force applied. Angle varies between PI/4 and PI/2 (the range has been halved since the setup of table is symmetric). The force varies between 200 and 2000 units (in p2 terms). In the plot, the angle varies on the X (horizontal) axis and the force varies on the Y (vertical) axis.
